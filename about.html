What Is Our Project About?

Our project is about identifying suspicious network/web traffic. Our program, Catalyst Web Sniffer (CWS) detects identifies suspicious packets based on many specially designed filters designed by Savvas.

The program works with Wireshark to identify suspicious packets. Meanwhile, the CWS also creates a .png file with the suspicious activity laid out graphically. A form of visualizition like a graph makes the data much easier to understand.
